# Product Overview

Petabytz is a Free and Open Source HRMS (Human Resource Management System) designed to streamline HR processes and enhance organizational efficiency.

## Core Features

- **Recruitment** - Candidate management and hiring workflows
- **Onboarding** - Employee onboarding processes
- **Employee Management** - Employee profiles and information management
- **Attendance Tracking** - Time tracking and attendance management
- **Leave Management** - Leave requests and approval workflows
- **Asset Management** - Company asset tracking and allocation
- **Payroll** - Salary processing and payroll management
- **Performance Management System (PMS)** - Employee performance tracking
- **Offboarding** - Employee exit processes
- **Helpdesk** - Internal support ticket system

## Additional Modules

- **Accessibility** - Accessibility features and compliance
- **Biometric Integration** - Support for biometric devices (Anviz, Cosec, Dahua, eTimeOffice)
- **Face Detection** - Facial recognition for attendance
- **Geofencing** - Location-based attendance tracking
- **Project Management** - Project tracking and management
- **Audit Trail** - System activity logging and auditing
- **Backup Management** - Database and file backup solutions
- **LDAP Integration** - Active Directory integration
- **Automations** - Workflow automation capabilities

## Target Users

HR departments and organizations looking for a comprehensive, open-source alternative to commercial HRMS solutions.
